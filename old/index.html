General Information

The Center City Slam is a 2000 meter indoor rowing race held at the Drexel University Armory. Events are held for men and women, from juniors through veterans.

Date/Registration

The 6th Annual Center City Slam will be held on

Saturday, February 9th 2013.

Location

The Drexel University Armory entrance is located on N. 33rd St. between Market and Arch steets.
